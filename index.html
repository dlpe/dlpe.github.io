🛠️ Support Page Content\n VolumeTracker Support Getting Started VolumeTracker is designed to be intuitive, but here are some quick tips to get you started: Adding Exercises: Use the "Add Exercise" button to create your first workout entry Tagging: Apply tags like "Dumbbells," "High," "Chest" to categorize your exercises Apple Watch: Install the companion Watch app for convenient workout logging Data Export: Access Settings > Data Export to share your workout data Frequently Asked Questions Q: How do I sync data between my iPhone and Apple Watch? A: Data automatically syncs through iCloud when both devices are connected to the internet. Q: Can I export my workout data? A: Yes! Go to Settings > Data Export to generate CSV or JSON files of your workouts. Q: How do I add custom tags? A: When selecting tags, simply type a new tag name and it will be automatically added to your available tags. Q: Is my data private? A: Absolutely. All data stays on your devices and syncs through your private iCloud account. We don't collect or store any personal information.
